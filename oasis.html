Overview

OASIS is an intelligent customer service solution designed for the OCBC Innovation Challenge. It leverages artificial intelligence to provide instant, personalized support for banking inquiries, seamlessly handles common customer queries, offers self-service routes, and intelligently escalates complex issues to human agents with full context preservation.

🎯 Project Objectives

Reduce Operational Load

Minimize physical branch and call center traffic by automating routine inquiry responses.

Enhance Satisfaction

Provide swift, personalized round-the-clock service via AI-powered assistance.

Empower Bank Staff

Deliver summary analysis of customer interactions to human agents for accelerated resolution.

✨ Key Features

🤖 AI-Powered Chatbot

Instant answers to common banking queries (balance checks, account opening guides, lost card reporting) powered by natural language understanding using Google Gemini AI.

🔄 Smart Escalation System

Automatic case transfer when queries exceed chatbot capabilities, preserving full conversation logs so customers never have to repeat themselves.

📱 QR Code Case Transfer

Unique QR code generation per case. Physical branch staff scan the QR code to instantly access case history, summaries, and sentiment metrics on mobile.

📊 Admin Dashboard

Real-time inquiry monitoring panel featuring sentiment analysis insights, automated case tracking status, and spreadsheet exporting features.
